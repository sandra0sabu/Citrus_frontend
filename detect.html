Our Story

Citrus farming is an essential part of agriculture, but diseases like Citrus Greening (Huanglongbing) often go unnoticed until serious damage has already occurred. Many farmers rely on visual inspection or expert visits, which can be time-consuming, costly, and not always available when needed

Our project was developed to support early identification of Citrus Greening through simple leaf image analysis. By allowing users to upload a photo of a citrus leaf, the system helps identify possible disease symptoms at an early stage. The aim is to make disease awareness quicker, easier, and more accessible for farmers and growers.

Early detection plays a key role in reducing crop loss and improving plant health. Through this platform, we hope to provide timely insights and practical guidance that help protect citrus crops and promote healthier, more sustainable farming practices.

We stand with citrus farmers everywhere by providing a simple and reliable platform for leaf disease detection. Our mission is to create an informed citrus farming community that encourages early action and sustainable crop care.

Symptoms



Citrus greening causes yellowing of leaves with blotchy, uneven patterns, misshapen bitter fruits, and overall decline of the tree.

Remedies



Remove infected trees, control psyllids, and use healthy plants.
